Blog

Success and Failure:

What do you think, what is the proper definition of Success and Failure and what should be the definition of these terms? Well “Failure is success in progress,” Albert Einstein once said. In this beautiful life everyone trying their best and giving 100% effort to get things happen. Every human being wants to lead a successful life, so they give their every sweat in their work to make it successful. At the end some people succeed some don’t. I believe for every individual there is nothing called failure. When someone starts a work from scratch or from the root level, if he gets success then its good for him and for society also if he doesn’t then for society he failed but for him he is not. Because when he started he was in Zero but after completion he failed in society but he gained something and learned something. That learning part no one can take from you. So in this world we are here to learn something and gain something. So I believe whatever we are doing right now is simple progress in life. We should not stop this in any situation.

Risk:

Every time human wants to do more than he is doing currently. Then he takes some steps to make that thought successful. He sacrifices everything , leaving all types of luxury and takes a risk to make it happen. Taking risk is a good progress in life, which we should try it, In this way world changes new things are generated, innovation increases to a good level. Risk is uncertain, i can’t be sure that if someone is taking then he will success. But you will learn many things and will try to implicate the next thought, one day you will reach that point which you had thought previously. Facebook founder always says if you are not taking any risk in life then it is the biggest risk you are taking.We shouldn’t fear while getting into a new thing, because fear decreases your confidence. If we are doing something then we need be confident and keep fear aside.

Entrepreneurship:

In this world of inaction over promising and under delivering every human being wants to do or wants to think out of the box. Then he starts focusing on that particular idea and gives his hundred percent to make it successful and achieve the goal. An entrepreneur creates an idea not with fully form, then being in the process of research and development, implements the idea into a product and present in the market place. Entrepreneurship means Learning, innovation and research.

 Entrepreneurs takes the risk and responsibility, implements the idea into a business model and starts business.
